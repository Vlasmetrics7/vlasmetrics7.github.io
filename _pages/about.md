I am an Associate Professor of Econometrics at the Instituto Tecnológico Autónomo de México (ITAM) and Visiting Professor at the Department of Economics, Duke University (2025–2026). 
I hold a PhD in Econometrics from Aarhus University and CREATES, Denmark.

My main research fields include:
- Time-series econometrics and long memory  
- High-dimensional data and forecasting  
- Macroeconometrics  
- Applied studies in financial econometrics, energy, climate, and macroeconomics  

I serve as Associate Editor of *Latin American Economic Review* (2023–2025). 
I am a member of the Mexican National System of Researchers (SNI, Level II).
